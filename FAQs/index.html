JULIA HAIR × CREATOR

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our creator collaboration program.

General Collaboration Questions

What type of collaboration is this?
This is a paid monthly influencer collaboration focused on video content creation featuring Julia Hair products across your social media platforms.
Is this a gifted or paid collaboration?
This is a paid collaboration. Compensation is issued per approved video, in addition to product provision.
How much is the payment?
Compensation is USD $800 per approved video, with four (4) videos per month, totaling USD $3,200 per month.
Is payment guaranteed?
Payment is guaranteed upon successful delivery and approval of agreed content, as outlined in the contract.
How often are payments made?
Payments are processed monthly after content submission and verification through the influencer portal.

Content & Deliverables

How many videos am I required to post monthly?
You are required to post four (4) videos per month, each featuring one Julia Hair wig or bundle.
What platforms should I post on?
Content should be published on your primary social media platform(s), typically Instagram and/or TikTok, unless otherwise agreed.
How long should the videos be?
Videos should typically range between 30–90 seconds, ensuring clear product visibility and natural presentation.
Can I reuse the same wig in multiple videos?
Each of the four required videos should feature a different wig or bundle unless otherwise approved.
Do posts need approval before publishing?
Formal pre-approval is not required unless stated, but all content must meet Julia Hair’s quality and brand standards to qualify for payment.

Product & Wig Allocation

How many wigs will I receive?
You will receive six (6) wigs or bundles per month.
How are the wigs allocated?
Four (4) wigs are designated for content creation and two (2) wigs are provided for your personal use.
Can I sell or give away the wigs?
No. Products are non-transferable and non-resale, as stated in the contract.
Can I choose the wigs myself?
Yes. You will select your wigs from the Julia Hair website, including preferred lengths and styles.

Shipping & Logistics

Who handles shipping?
Shipping is coordinated through FedEx.
Who pays for shipping?
Shipping is coordinated and paid directly between the creator and FedEx. Julia Hair does not process shipping payments.
Why do I need to contact SwiftShip myself?
SwiftShip manages destination confirmation, delivery scheduling, and tracking issuance directly with creators for accuracy and efficiency.
How does FedEx know which items to ship?
Your shipment details are prepared and allocated internally. When contacting FedEx, reference:

“Julia Hair Collaboration – [Your Full Name]”
Will I receive tracking information?
Yes. Tracking details are issued by FedEx once shipment is scheduled and dispatched.
What happens if I delay shipment coordination?
Delays in shipping coordination may result in postponed dispatch and campaign timelines.

Payment & Portal Questions

How do I receive my payments?
Payments are issued via your selected payout method inside the influencer portal.
Can I change my payout method later?
Yes. Payout methods can be updated within the portal before payment processing.
Is shipping deducted from my payment?
No. Shipping is handled separately and is not deducted from creator compensation.
What is the influencer portal used for?
Content submission, payment tracking, payout method selection, and campaign records.

Legal & Contract Questions

Why do I need to sign a contract?
The contract protects both parties by clearly defining expectations, payments, timelines, and usage rights.
Am I an employee of Julia Hair?
No. You are an independent contractor.
Can I work with other brands?
Yes — however, promotion of competing hair brands requires prior written consent.
What happens if I miss a posting deadline?
Communicate delays promptly. Repeated or unnotified delays may affect payment eligibility.
What happens if I receive products but fail to post?
Payments may be withheld and the campaign may be terminated.
Can Julia Hair use my content elsewhere?
Yes. You grant Julia Hair a non-exclusive, royalty-free license to use your content for marketing.

Professional Conduct & Support

Who do I contact if I have questions?
Contact your assigned Julia Hair representative or use the influencer portal support channel.
Is this collaboration long-term?
This collaboration is monthly and may be renewed based on performance and mutual interest.
What makes this collaboration successful?
Clear communication, timeliness, authenticity, and professionalism.

Final Confirmation

What confirms my participation officially?
Contract signing, shipment coordination, and influencer portal submission.
